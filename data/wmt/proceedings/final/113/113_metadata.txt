Abstract#==#In this report paper we propose three different methods for automatic
evaluation of the machine translation (MT) quality. Two of the metrics are
trainable on direct-assessment scores and two of them use dependency
structures. The trainable metric AutoDA, which uses deep-syntactic features,
achieved better correlation with humans compared e.g. to the chrF3 metric.
