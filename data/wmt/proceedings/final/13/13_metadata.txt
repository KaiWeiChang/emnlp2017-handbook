Abstract#==#In this paper, we describe the TALP-UPC participation in the News Task for
German-English and Finish-English. Our primary submission implements a fully
character to character neural machine translation architecture with an
additional rescoring of a n-best list of hypothesis using a forced
back-translation to the source sentence. This model gives consistent
improvements on different pairs of languages for the language direction with
the low-est performance while keeping the quality in the direction with the
highest performance.
Additional experiments are reported for multilingual character to character
neural machine translation, phrase-based translation and the additional
Turkish-English language pair.
