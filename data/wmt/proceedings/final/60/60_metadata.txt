Abstract#==#This paper describes the AFRL machine translation systems and the improvements
that were developed during the WMT17 evaluation campaign. This year, we explore
the continuing proliferation of Neural Machine Translation toolkits, revisit
our previous data-selection efforts for use in training systems with these
new toolkits and expand our participation to the Russian–English,
Turkish–English and Chinese–English translation pairs.
