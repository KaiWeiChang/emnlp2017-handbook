Abstract#==#This paper describes the LMU Munich English-to-German machine translation
systems. We participated with neural translation engines in the WMT17 shared
task on machine translation of news, as well as in the biomedical translation
task. LMU Munich's systems deliver competitive machine translation quality on
both news articles and health information texts.
